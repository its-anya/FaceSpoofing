About Face Spoofing Detection

This application uses computer vision and machine learning techniques to detect face spoofing attempts during video calls. It can identify various spoofing methods including:

  • Printed photos
  • Digital screen displays
  • Video replays
  • 3D masks (with limitations)

The system analyzes texture patterns, color variations, and reflection properties to distinguish between real faces and spoofing attempts.
